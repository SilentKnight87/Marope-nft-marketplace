Set up your wallet

Once you’ve set up your wallet of choice, connect it to Marope by clicking the wallet icon in the top right corner.

Create your collection

Click Collection and set up your collection. Add social links, profile & banner images, and set a secondary sales fee.

Add your NFTs

Upload your work (music, books, art), add a title and description, and customize your NFTs with properties, and unlockable content.

List them for sale

Choose fixed-price, and we help you sell them!
