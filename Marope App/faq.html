Frequently Ask Questions & Help center

How to start as a seller?

First you need to connect to metamaskt wallet, then go to upload page. Upload your file and fill out the fields of the form (name, description, price). Once complete upload file by clicking on create item button.

When and how do you get paid?

Once your item has been sold, the funds will be transfer from the buyer's account to the metamask seller's account with a Ethereum currency.

How much can you earn?

It depends on the value price of the nft asset or item has been set to sell.

What is the service fee for sellers?

The service is 2.5% that goes to Marope marketplace once the item or asset is sold.

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for non-fungible token, a Non-fungible token is an economic term use to describe things like furniture, assets, a song file. NFTs are tokens use to represent ownership of unique items. It lets tokenise things such as art, collectibles, and assets. They can only have one official owner at a time and they're secured by the Ethereum blockchain.

Which crypto currency does Marope offers?

Marope only accepts Ethereum currency.

What are NFT gas fees?

Gas fees are like transaction fees on the Ethereum blockchain. When you make transactions, such as transfering crypto to another wallet or purchasing an NFT on Marope, you'll need enough ETH in your wallet for the initial transaction and the associated gas fees.

What happens when there are not enough funds to purchase?

When attempting to purschase an item or asset metamask will notify if account has enough funds to make purchase.

When will I receive my NFT?

Once purchase trasaction has been succesful, the item will be display in your metamask wallet.
